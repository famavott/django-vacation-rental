At 'Daybreak on Sunset' enjoy the beautiful panoramic views of the ocean while you are relaxing in our beachfront home anytime of the year. Daybreak on Sunset is wonderful for a girls getaway, celebrating special occasions or just getting out of town to relax after a hard work week with the ones you love. There are no roads to cross, and just a few private steps down off the deck until you are walking on the beach. Gorgeous views on an unspoiled quiet beach are ready for your enjoyment. Daybreak on Sunset can comfortably sleep 1-6 people. Upstairs you will have a breathtaking view from our large master loft bedroom with a queen bed with a full bathroom. Downstairs there are two bedrooms and a full bathroom. One room has a queen size bed, while the other has a full size bed along with a twin bed.
